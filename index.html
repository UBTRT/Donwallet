📖 Frequently Asked Questions
❓What is DON-PESSA?›
DON-PESSA is a smart investment platform that allows you to grow your money by earning 10% daily returns on your deposited amount for 60 days. You can also earn referral bonuses by inviting friends to join.
❓How do I deposit?›
Go to Deposit, select your network (MTN or Airtel), enter your phone number and amount, then click Submit. You will receive a USSD prompt on your phone — approve it with your mobile money PIN. Your balance is credited automatically.
❓How much can I earn daily?›
You earn 10% of your deposited amount every day for 60 days. Example: deposit UGX 100,000 → earn UGX 10,000/day → total UGX 600,000 after 60 days.
❓When are daily profits credited?›
Daily profits are automatically credited to your Balance every 24 hours. You also get an instant 10% profit when your deposit is confirmed.
❓What is the minimum deposit and withdrawal?›
Minimum deposit is set by admin (shown on the deposit page). Minimum withdrawal is UGX 5,000. A service charge is deducted on every withdrawal.
❓How does the referral program work?›
When someone registers using your referral link and deposits, you earn: Level 1 (direct) 10%, Level 2 earns 4%, Level 3 earns 1% — credited to your Balance instantly.
❓How long does a withdrawal take?›
Withdrawals are processed automatically via mobile money. Funds are sent directly to your registered number within minutes.
❓Is my money safe?›
DON-PESSA is committed to the security of all user funds. All transactions go through verified mobile money channels. Contact support for any concerns.
