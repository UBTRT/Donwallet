📖 Frequently Asked Questions
❓What is DON-WALLET?›
DON-WALLET is a smart investment platform that allows you to grow your money by earning 10% daily returns on your deposited amount for 60 days. You can also earn referral bonuses by inviting friends to join.
❓How do I start investing?›
Register an account, go to Deposit, enter at least UGX 10,000 and your mobile money number, click SUBMIT and confirm the payment. Your investment activates after admin approval.
❓How much can I earn daily?›
You earn 10% of your deposited amount every day for 60 days. Example: deposit UGX 100,000 → earn UGX 10,000/day → total UGX 600,000 after 60 days.
❓When are daily profits credited?›
Daily profits are automatically credited to your Balance every 24 hours. You also get an instant 10% profit when your deposit is approved.
❓What is the minimum deposit and withdrawal?›
Minimum deposit is UGX 10,000. Minimum withdrawal is UGX 5,000. A 10% service charge is deducted on every withdrawal.
❓How does the referral program work?›
When someone registers using your referral link and deposits, you earn: Level 1 (direct) 10%, Level 2 earns 4%, Level 3 earns 1% — credited to your Balance instantly.
❓Can I withdraw my referral bonuses?›
Yes, but only after activating your account by depositing or re-investing at least UGX 10,000.
❓What is Re-Invest?›
Re-Invest moves funds from your Balance to Invested, starting a new 60-day profit cycle. Minimum UGX 10,000, plus you get instant 10% profit.
❓How long does a withdrawal take?›
Withdrawals are reviewed by admin and processed quickly. Funds are sent to your registered mobile money number. Contact support if delayed.
❓Is my money safe?›
DON-WALLET is committed to the security of all user funds. All transactions go through verified mobile money channels. Contact support for any concerns.
